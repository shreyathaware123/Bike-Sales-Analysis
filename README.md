# 🚵 Bike Sales Analysis Dashboard (Excel Project)

This project presents a comprehensive sales and customer segmentation analysis using Microsoft Excel. The dashboard evaluates purchasing behavior, demographic trends, commute patterns, and income distribution to identify key drivers influencing bike sales.

## ➡️ Project Objective

To analyze customer data and uncover insights related to:

* Purchase behavior patterns
* Demographic influence on sales
* Income vs commute trends
* Regional performance
* Occupation-based income distribution
* Homeownership and marital status impact on purchases

The goal is to support data-driven marketing strategies and customer targeting decisions.

## ➡️ Key Insights from Dashboard

###  Sales by Region

* North America shows the highest sales (508 orders)
* Europe and Pacific follow with lower purchase volumes
* Regional demand differences highlight market concentration

###  Income vs Commute Distance

* Customers commuting 10+ miles show higher income levels
* 2–5 mile commuters also demonstrate strong income distribution
* Short-distance commuters show relatively lower income brackets

This suggests commute distance may correlate with purchasing power.

###  Income by Occupation

* Management roles have the highest income index (153.74%)
* Professionals follow (133.20%)
* Manual roles show comparatively lower income levels

Occupation significantly influences purchasing capacity.

###  Sales by Homeownership

* 683 homeowners purchased bikes
* Only 317 non-homeowners made purchases

Homeownership status strongly impacts buying behavior.

###  Gender Breakdown

* Male buyers: 511
* Female buyers: 489

Relatively balanced gender distribution.

###  Age Analysis

* Adult segment dominates purchases
* Young customers show moderate buying behavior
* Older customers contribute less to overall sales

###  Marital Status Share

* Married customers represent the larger share (538)
* Single customers account for 462 purchases

Marital status influences purchasing decisions.
